Shipping Policy

Last Updated: 24/09/2025

General Information

At TheNearByShop we aim to deliver your daily essential needs quickly and safely. This Shipping Policy explains how we handle orders, shipping timelines, and delivery methods.

Order Processing
  • Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation (excluding Sundays and public holidays).
  • You will receive an email or SMS notification once your order has been shipped.
  • If an item is on backorder or unavailable, we will notify you promptly and offer alternatives or a refund.
Shipping Methods & Delivery Time
  • We ship through reputed courier partners (such as Delhivery, Blue Dart, DTDC, etc.) depending on your location.
  • Estimated delivery time within India is usually 3–7 business days after dispatch.
  • Delivery timelines may vary for remote or out-of-service areas.
Shipping Charges
  • Shipping charges are displayed at checkout before you place your order.
  • We may offer free shipping promotions from time to time, which will be mentioned on the product page or during checkout.
Tracking Your Order
  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number and link via email/SMS.
  • You can track your shipment on the courier partner’s website until it reaches you.
Address Accuracy
  • Please ensure your shipping address, phone number, and pin code are accurate.
  • We are not responsible for delays or failed deliveries caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer.
Delivery Attempts
  • Our courier partners will make up to two attempts to deliver your package.
  • If delivery fails due to customer unavailability or incorrect address, the order may be returned to us. Re-shipping charges may apply.
International Shipping
  • Currently, we do not offer international shipping. Orders are delivered within India only. (You can update this if you plan to ship globally.)
Damaged or Lost Packages
  • If you receive a damaged package, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the packaging and item.
  • We will investigate and assist with replacements/refunds as per our Return Policy.
  • In rare cases of lost shipments, we will coordinate with the courier to resolve the issue or refund you.

Returns & Refunds Policy

  • Physical items may be returned or exchanged within 3 days of delivery if defective, damaged, or incorrect, subject to our return policy.
  • Digital products (eBooks) are non-returnable and non-refundable once downloaded.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method after inspection/approval.
