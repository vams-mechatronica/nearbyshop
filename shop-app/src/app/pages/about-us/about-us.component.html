About Us

TheNearByShop is a local commerce and neighbourhood shopping platform designed to connect customers with nearby stores and service providers for faster, more convenient deliveries.

TheNearByShop is a product and brand owned and operated by VAMS Mechatronica Private Limited, a technology-driven company specializing in advanced mechatronics and digital solutions.

VAMS Mechatronica Private Limited is committed to building reliable, efficient, and scalable technology platforms across manufacturing, automation, and consumer-focused digital services.
